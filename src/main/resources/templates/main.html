Welcome to SQLad's Goodreads Book & Author Recommendation Tool!

Using our search tools, users can discover various authors and books and their associated ratings and tags on Goodreads. This data was collected from the Goodbooks database, which contains the top 10,000 books listed on Goodreads as well as user tags and reviews related to each book.

Top Books: In our Top Books page users can see the top 100 rated books on Goodreads. This was determined based off of overall user review score and number of reviews.

Top Authors: After looking at the highest rated books on Goodreads and how many people reviewed them, we collected the authors with the most highly rated books and collected them in one page for users to read through.

Bucket List: Users on Goodreads have a lot of books they want to read. After looking at 25,000 records of books people wanted to read, here are the ones people want to read the most!

Search: Looking for something more specific? Use our search tool to look through keywords and tags to find the book you're looking for.

Happy searching!
